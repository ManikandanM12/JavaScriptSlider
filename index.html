Switzerland
Switzerland's nature is a breathtaking blend of majestic Alps, crystal-clear lakes, and lush green meadows.
Matterhorn
One of the most iconic peaks in the world, the Matterhorn towers at 4,478 meters on the border between Switzerland and Italy.
Lake Geneva
Stretching along the border of Switzerland and France, Lake Geneva is one of Europe’s largest freshwater lakes.
Lauterbrunnen Valley
Known as the "Valley of 72 Waterfalls," Lauterbrunnen is a picturesque landscape with towering cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and charming alpine meadows.
Interlaken
Nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, Interlaken is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Surrounded by the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau mountains.
